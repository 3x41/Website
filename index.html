I am Alex and this site was made to demonstrate some of the work that I have created over the years and to show some of the things I am still working on. Please feel free to have a look around.

I work within the IT industry and I am a qualified Computer Software Developer (BSc). I have had many different roles in the past. Including System Administrator, Technical Support, Laptop/PC Repair, Teaching Programming, Programming for different companies, Wiring up networks (Cat5e/Fibre) and network diagnostics to name just a few.

In the winter I try to stay in the warm and in more recent years worked on repairing electrical items. I have also just built a 3D printer and mini laser engraver for the little projects I try and work on.

I also sometimes upload videos to YouTube.
